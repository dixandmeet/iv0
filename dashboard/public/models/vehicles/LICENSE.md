# Vehicle model assets

The `bus.glb`, `tram.glb`, and `car.glb` files are converted from the
**Public Transport Pack** by Quaternius.

- Source: https://quaternius.com/packs/publictransport.html
- Original models: `Bus.obj`, `Train.obj`, and `Taxi.obj`
- License: Creative Commons Zero v1.0 Universal (CC0-1.0)
- License text: https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/

The source pack permits personal and commercial use. The OBJ models were
converted to binary glTF for this application; geometry was not otherwise
redistributed from another source.
